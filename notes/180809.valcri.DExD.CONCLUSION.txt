--- CONCLUSION --

DExD is an approach
enabled by emerging technologies
fits in well with broader methods - DSM, agile

Enables us to achieve MI and RS

Compliments notebooks, but is different in that it focuses on establishing and addressing problems through visualization design and is explicit about design decisions in doing so.
We have implemented some dialogue devices - there is scope for expanding and assessing these, and implementing others.

Can be created in various ways, we provide some options.

May lead to different designs in terms of family, sophistication, breadth.

We offer some evidence to support these contentions based upon a long term study that has resulted in software installation in our collaborators' organization. DExD separation from study

We call for this approach to be explored, enhanced, more fully (broadened, tested, etc.)
